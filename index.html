CYBERCROSSWORD

Test your cybersecurity knowledge!

📋 How to Play

🎯 What is this?

This is a cybersecurity crossword puzzle. Your goal is to fill in all the blank squares using the clues provided. Every answer is a cybersecurity term — no general knowledge needed beyond your security know-how!

🖱️ Filling in answers
  • Click any highlighted square to select it. The full word it belongs to will light up in teal.
  • ACROSS words run left-to-right ➡️. DOWN words run top-to-bottom ⬇️. The clues panel on the right tells you which is which.
  • Just start typing — each letter fills the selected square and the cursor moves automatically to the next square in the word.
  • Click the same square twice to switch between typing ACROSS or DOWN if the square belongs to two words.
  • Use arrow keys to move between squares, Backspace to erase, and Tab to jump to the next word.
  • You can also click any clue in the panel to jump straight to that word on the grid.
  • When you complete a word correctly, the squares turn green automatically — no submit button needed!
⚡ Scoring — how points work
  • Base points: Every correct letter in a word earns you 10 points. A 6-letter word = 60 pts, a 12-letter word = 120 pts.
  • Time bonus: Each word also carries a bonus of up to 300 points, which counts down by 1 point every second from the moment you start. Solve a word at the 1-minute mark and you get a 240-point bonus on top. Solve it after 5 minutes and the bonus is zero — but you still earn the base points.
  • Example: Solve an 8-letter word at the 2-minute mark → 80 pts (base) + 180 pts (bonus) = 260 pts for that word.
  • Maximum possible score is around 5,580 pts (if you could solve every word instantly). A realistic top score for a fast player is around 4,000–4,500 pts.
📌 Important rules
  • ⏱️ Your timer starts the moment you click LET'S GO — not when you type your first letter.
  • 🔄 Switching browser tabs pauses your timer but flags your entry as suspicious. Three or more tab switches will mark you on the leaderboard.
  • 🚫 One attempt per Employee ID. Once you start, you cannot restart under the same ID. Make it count!
  • 🏆 Final rankings are determined by total score first, then by fastest completion time as a tiebreaker.
